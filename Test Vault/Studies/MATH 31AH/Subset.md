- $\subset$ : "is a [[subset]] of" (or equals, when $A \subset A$)
> Example:
> $E_{15}$ =  all natural numbers divisible by 15
> $E_{3}$ =  all natural numbers divisible by 3

>Proof: Prove that $E_{15} \subset E_{3}$
> $p \in E_{15}$
> $\frac{15}{p}$
> There exists another natural number $k$ such that 
> $p=15k$
> but $\frac{3}{15k}=p$
> Thus, $p \in E_{3}$
