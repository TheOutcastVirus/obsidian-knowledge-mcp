> Definition: Transpose is the operation that interchanges the rows and columns of a matrix. The transpose of $A$ is denoted as $A^T$

> Example: Say you have a matrix $A = \begin{bmatrix}0 & 1 & 2 \\ 1 & 2 & 3\end{bmatrix} \in M_{2 \times 3}(\mathbb{R})$. The the tranpose would be $A^T=\begin{bmatrix}0 & 1 \\ 1 & 2  \\ 2 & 3\end{bmatrix} \in M_{3 \times 2}(\mathbb{R})$. 
- Transpose is commutative $(A+B)^T = A^T +B^T$
- $(AB)^T = B^TA^T$
